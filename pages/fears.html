Fear Thesaurus

Many things contribute to who a character becomes, but it is their ability (or failure) to conquer core fears that often determines their fate in the story. Use this thesaurus to uncover the fears that are holding your character back so you can write their behavior, show internal struggles, and include triggers that chip away at their disguise, helping them break free of the internal blocks that stand in the way of success.
