VIGILANTE ⚹
Back to Character Types and Tropes

Description:

Disillusioned by an establishment, as they take the law into their own hands and punish wrongdoers themselves. These characters have often experienced a grave injustice that drives them to seek vengeance. Despite their actions being outside of the law, their activities help others.
