SUPERVILLAIN

Helpful Tip: Using a trope or archetype helps readers quickly understand a character, but stepping them beyond their predictable layers: beyond, add depth with a unique backstory, emotional wound, and related trait and flaw. You can also break expectations with an internal job, informative ways, or positive personality change that will help your character stand apart.

Description:

This megalomaniac nemesis is augmented with extraordinary or supernatural powers. There is typically a history between the two characters to explain and complicate their dynamic.

Fictional Examples:

Lex Luthor (the Superman franchise), Voldemort (Harry Potter series), Magneto (X-Men movies, the Joker (the Batman franchise), Loki (Thor)
