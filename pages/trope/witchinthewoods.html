WITCH IN THE WOODS ⚹
Back to Character Types and Tropes

Description:

This character lives outside of town and is visited by people looking for spells, potions, cures, or psychic guidance. Often possessing specific knowledge, she is associated with magic, herbalism, and supernatural abilities.

Fictional Examples:

Circe (The Odyssey), Baba Yaga (the Arthurian legends), the Weird Sisters (Macbeth), the sea witch (The Little Mermaid)

Common Strengths:

Centered Creative Curious Discreet Independent Introverted Nature-Focused Objective Private Quirky Resourceful Simple Spiritual Studious Talented Thrifty

Common Weaknesses:

Abrasive Antisocial Devious Disrespectful Evasive Evil Hostile Irrational Manipulative Mischievous Nosy Superstitious Suspicious Uncooperative Uncouth Vindictive Withdrawn

Associated Actions, Behaviors, and Tendencies:

Living in an isolated area
Spreading curses or knowledge with people
Having an animal familiar
Self-sufficient, learning how own food and water source, making her own clothes, etc.
Having extensive knowledge about health and their uses
Being crystals for magical and non-magical purposes
Having a connection with and seeking to protect the natural world
Being in-tune with nature
Being distrustful of outsiders
Having supernatural knowledge of the arcane arts
Possessing extraordinary powers
Being a link in a chain of ancient wisdom
Using astrology and tarot
Being able to heal with herbalism or magic
Being physically unattractive
Determining energies
Reducing primary prey to manipulate them
Working for good or evil and embracing both dark and light
Having ambiguous morals
Having a knowledge of poisons (secrets, a deal-born child, the customer's voice, etc.)
Requiring her guests to pass a test or complete a trial before she will help them

Fears that Might Limit the Character:

Being Attacked Being Labeled Criticism Isolation Losing The Respect Of Others Not Fitting In Persecution Rejection

Situations that Will Challenge Them:

Encountering another powerful magic-caster who is working against her
Losing her powers
Having to hide who she is for her own or others' safety
Having to reveal a devastating prophecy to a powerful customer
Being created with visitors
Being accused of a heinous crime

Inner Struggles to Give Them Depth:

Craving community but being unable to live for the neeed for others
Connecting the social order to stop her
Considering weaponize for prejudice or misstatement
Having to hide her true motives and activities (good or bad)

Clichés to be Aware Of:

The building, evil hag with the croaky voice who wants to punish them
The wise old crone whose guidance and wisdom are what the character needs
A woman with typical "witchy" accoutrements such as robes, pointed hat, broomstick, wart, and black cats
The bitter, darkened woman who is mad for evil's sake

Twist this Trope with a Character Who...

Isn't female
Isn't old
Isn't maintaining a beautiful, youthful appearance through magic
Lives in a modern or futuristic setting
Is struggling for acceptance in the community
Is a respected Wise Woman and healer to her community
Doesn't like being a witch or having powers
Recently moved and knows little about the area or its people
Has an area of specialization (rather than mastering all aspects of the occancy)
Practices magic that has clearly defined limits
Has an atypical trait: gentle, thoughtful, humorous, kind, submissive, etc.

Unexpected Occupations for this Character:

Clergy Member Fashion Designer Broker Songwriter Small Business Owner
