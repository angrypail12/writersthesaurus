GOOD KING OR QUEEN
Using a trope or archetype helps readers quickly understand a character. But mapping them outside of typical expectations helps characters stand out and become memorable, not stereotypical. When used with purpose, every trope can become a compelling character.

Description

The good ruler is moral and well-being of their people. They exist to govern with fairness and compassion.

Fictional Examples

King Arthur (The Once and Future King), Queen Padme Amidala (Star Wars Episodes), Rey T'Challa (Black Panther) (Bio Goku) (Bio: War for the Planet)
