Associated Actions, Behaviors, and Tendencies:

  • Displaying authority and leadership
  • A powerful sense of duty
  • Having control over every aspect of work
  • Being somewhat risk-averse and acting with caution
  • Always thinking about the big picture
  • Planning ahead
  • Respecting traditions and customs, understanding their importance
  • Viewing every interaction as a way to reinforce alliances, secure bonds, and further goals
  • Being in control of their environment (what happens and when)
  • Being direct when communicating (and expecting orders to be followed without question)
  • Being forceful and direct
  • Taking ownership of failures and mistakes and rarely making the same mistake twice
  • Being very good at mediating emotions
  • Taking a back and being comfortable with conflict
  • Perfectionism, tendencies and having high expectations of others
  • Maintaining appearances even in times of stress to never appear weak or not in control
  • Sacrificing personal desires for what is best for the group
  • Having a defined set of values and following them (and expecting others to also)
  • Relying on trusted agents who help private themselves loyal over family or close
  • Living in a way that maintains their reputation (be that a fair ruler, an unforgiving one, etc.)
  • Following through on promises, threats, and consequences
  • Holding themselves to the highest standards
  • Having low close relationships
  • Rarely (or never) letting down their guard to reveal insecurities or worries
  • Being mindful of their reputation and appearance
  • Being able to make the hard decisions when necessary
  • Analyzing a) the odds and b) following them (and expecting others to also)
  • Adapting a "the ends justify the means" mentality at times

Situations That Will Challenge Them:

  • Needing to choose between duty and love
  • Having to rely on someone or something that is unpredictable
  • A chaotic environment where structures and order is disrupted
  • Hard choices that go against their moral beliefs
  • Having a personal vulnerability become public
  • Loved ones facing a dangerous situation the character's resources can't save them from
  • Being challenged by a competitor
  • Measures that require patience
  • Having to make a decision that brings their loyalty to country or family first
  • Needing information to make decisions that impact others and not having it
  • A cognitive challenge that impairs memory or the ability to make clear decisions
  • Being asked to put personal welfare first (needs like balance, tending to personal relationships, etc.)
  • When someone knows their secrets or has a form of leverage

Inner Struggles to Give Them Depth:

  • Feeling anxiety and doubt and being unable to show it or confide in anyone
  • Feeling emotionally isolated and yet being unable to take the personal risk of vulnerability that faith
  • Feeling the more they sacrifice a risk, the more they lose their sense of self
  • Wanting to pursue something but knowing they cannot because of their duty, a custom, expectations, etc. (like choosing whom to marry, for friends with, or whom to love)
  • Struggling with loneliness and having little to no opportunities for self-care
  • Being terrified of failing or making a mistake that harms those they swore to protect
  • Feeling loved ones face hatred and love because she's been hurt before
  • Struggling with understanding that despite their best efforts, they cannot
