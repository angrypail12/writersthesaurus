Situations That Will Challenge Them:

  • Facing a villain who learns each weakness or is stronger than they are
  • Being betrayed by someone they trust
  • Their true identity being revealed
  • Having their powers stripped from them or come collateral damage
  • Their powers failing at the worst possible time
  • Losing their powers
  • Being forced to work with people who are incompetent or have a different belief system
  • Loved ones facing a dangerous situation the character's powers can't save them from
  • Being forced to choose between duty and their family
  • Having to remove someone who is insanely evil
  • Having to join forces with a rival

Inner Struggles to Give Them Depth:

  • Feeling isolated because they can't tell people the truth about their powers or their identity
  • Feeling like they don't belong in the regular (human) world
  • Fearing that their powers aren't strong enough to combat the evil they're facing
  • Fearing that using great power will corrupt them or change who they are
  • Feeling overwhelmed by the magnitude of the task they're facing
  • Wanting to give up
  • Worrying that the people they're saving aren't worth it
  • Being frustrated when the people they save end up back in trouble
