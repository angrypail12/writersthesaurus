Knight Errant

Using a trope or archetype can provide a recognizable starting point, but each character must be fully-fleshed out to your page. Refer to The Character Type and Trope Thesaurus for a wealth of story-building descriptive details to help bring your characters to life in your stories.

Description

The knight is searching for adventure and quests through which they can show their merit, highlight their ideals, and right wrongs. While this trope is popular in fantasy stories, its associated qualities and experiences can be applied to characters in any genre.

Fictional Examples

Percival Frey (Game of Thrones), Boromir of Gondor (The Lord of the Rings), Don Quixote (Don Quixote), Roland Deschain (The Dark Tower)
