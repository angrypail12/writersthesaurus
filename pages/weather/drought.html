Reinforcing a Mood

Water is essential to life, so when rain refuses to fall and sources of water dry up, society sets in. A drought will often create a mood of desperation and hopelessness as people struggle to find water and come to grips with the lack of control over this important need in their lives.

Symbolism

DEPRESSION, STAGNATION, DOOMSDAY, INJUSTICE, ISOLATION, SUFFERING, FAILURE, HOPELESSNESS, SURVIVAL OF THE FITTEST, BARRENNESS, DESPERATION

Weather Notes

A drought is caused by a climatic event or circumstances that drop temperatures increased evaporation and generated sweeping waterfronts. But in areas where there are no water reserves, the reality of prolonged droughts can be devastating, leading to long-term consequences, social, and environmental problems.

Scenarios for Adding Conflict or Tension

  • Characters becoming desperate to find water (causing an upheaval in problems thinking, failing, the extraction of certain metals or minerals, etc.)
  • Dangerous animals growing frantic because one threatening food and water sources are depleted
  • Severe dust allergies or skin ailments being amplified by a drought's conditions
