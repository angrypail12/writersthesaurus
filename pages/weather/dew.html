Dew

Sights

  • Beads of water on flat surfaces
  • A wet crystalline shimmer on greenery and trees
  • Clear droplets collecting in the center of flower cups or leaf points so stems
  • Droplets pooling together, subdivided and polishing out plant
  • A healthy gleam to natural foliage
  • Grass that appears frosted
  • Heavy water pearls collecting on the sides of rain barrels or a glass patio table
  • Sparkles that slip distorted surfaces in heavy droplets run together
  • Water trails from leaves or objects in the garden
  • Human feet or animal tracks making a clear path through a dewy field

Smells

  • A fresh moist scent
  • Greenery and growing things

Tastes

Water lacking a faint taste of the surface it has touched (the metallic tang of metal or rust, for example)

Textures and Sensations

  • Dampness
  • Wetness
  • Cool water settling against one's legs or walking through pool calls and shoes
  • Wet droplets shaking loose from leaves above and falling on one's skin
  • Brushing against surfaces that transfer moisture to one's clothes or skin
  • Air that feels heavy and moist
  • Becoming damp despite one's best efforts
  • Damp clothing or towels that cress back and overnight

Reinforcing a Mood

Dew reinforces feelings of calm and stillness. Most noticeable in the morning, it transitions night to day in a theme of renewal.

Symbolism

PURITY, BEGINNINGS, HEALING, CLEANSING

Common Clichés

Comparing dew to jewels or tears

Weather Notes

Dew gathers in the late evening hours when temperatures are at their coolest and the air contains the highest amount of moisture.

Scenarios for Adding Conflict or Tension

  • Dew creating slippery surfaces that someone the character's goal or warning
  • A character feeling uncomfortable from the dampness despite other nice weather and having it ruined by dew
  • Having one's escape route detected because dew is present
